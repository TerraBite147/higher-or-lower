RULES

The Objective of Higher or Lower is to guess whether the next card is higher or lower than the current one to earn points.

Each correct guess doubles your score (1, 2, 4, etc.), but an incorrect guess resets your streak to zero.
Bank your points to secure them.

Reach 50 points to win.

Restart the game anytime to start fresh.

Enjoy testing your card-guessing skills and aiming for victory!
